# Handyman FAQ: Jose Does It LLC

## Company Overview
**What is Jose Does It LLC?**  
Jose Does It is a handyman service named after its creator, Jose.

**What services does Jose Does It LLC offer?**  
Jose Does It offers TV installation, furniture assembly, closet remodels, DIY projects and various other home renovation projects. See our services page for more details.

**What types of flooring do you install?**  
Jose Does It installs LVP flooring (Luxury Vinyl Plank) and various types of floor tile.

## Booking, Payment, and Insurance
**How can I book a service with Jose Does It?**  
You can book a service using Acuity booking on our website or over the phone. We prefer online bookings.

**How do I pay for a service with Jose Does It?**  
You can pay in advance with a credit or debit card using Stripe. Any additional time added to the job will be added to the final invoice.

**Do you carry COI?**  
Yes! We have General liability insurance.

## Capabilities and Assembly
**WHAT IF SOMETHING DOESN'T COME WITH INSTRUCTIONS?**  
No worries! My skills go beyond following directions. Whether it comes with instructions or not, I can assemble it. I can use my knowledge of common assembly techniques and logical deduction to figure things out.

**Can you mount things on walls, like TVs or shelves?**  
Absolutely, we have tons of experience mounting TV’s, Large shelves and Heavy mirrors. See our gallery for inspiration.

**What if I'm unsure if you can assemble something?**  
Feel free to describe the project to me! I can usually give you a good idea of whether I can handle it based on the available information (pictures, descriptions, etc.). You can also check this list of furniture pieces to see if your piece is listed / plan your appointment.

**Do you offer furniture assembly services?**  
You bet! I can expertly assemble any furniture, from flat-pack boxes to more intricate pieces. Looking to personalize a piece? I can also help with furniture customization, such as adding shelves, repainting, or replacing hardware.

**Do you build decks and sheds?**  
Absolutely! With plenty of successful projects under my belt, I have the experience and expertise to bring your outdoor vision to life. See this chart for pricing.
