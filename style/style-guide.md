# JoseDoesIt Visual Style Guide (for Astro + Tailwind rebuild)

## Overall Aesthetic
- Clean, professional, minimalist handyman service site.
- Heavy use of white space.
- High-quality, bright, well-lit project photography in grids (masonry or 2-4 column responsive).
- Simple, trustworthy, local feel (Queens/Brooklyn/Long Island focus).
- No clutter. Bold headings. Clear CTAs.

## Color Palette (approximated from site)
- Background: Pure white (#ffffff)
- Text: Dark charcoal / near black (#1a1a1a or #222)
- Accents / Buttons: Likely dark navy or black for borders and CTAs (phone button in header is outlined).
- Logo: Black text or simple icon.
- Photos: Natural colors, blues from outdoor furniture in hero examples.

## Typography
- Sans-serif (system or similar to Squarespace default — clean like Inter or Helvetica Neue).
- Headings: Bold, often uppercase or strong weight, larger sizes (e.g. 2xl-4xl for main).
- Body: Regular weight, good line height, readable.
- Strong emphasis on certain words in headings/features.

## Layout & Spacing
- Generous padding/margins (lots of breathing room).
- Top nav: Simple horizontal links (Home, Services, FAQ, Price Guide), centered or left logo, right phone number as button/link.
- Hero: Large image or video background, overlaid bold headline + CTA button.
- Feature blocks: 3-column on home (On Time & Local, Clear Pricing, Quality Work) with icons or just text + strong subhead.
- Galleries: Photo grids with lightbox feel, descriptive intro text above.
- Testimonials: Carousel or grid of quotes with names.
- Price tables: Clean, bordered or striped tables with service + time estimates.
- Footer: Centered "JOSE DOES IT! LLC", social links (Instagram, TikTok), email, phone.

## Buttons & CTAs
- Simple outlined or filled buttons.
- Phone number prominent in header (clickable).
- "SCHEDULE YOUR APPOINTMENT" or "Book" CTAs leading to Acuity embed.
- "CLICK HERE TO BOOK>>>" on services page.

## Images
- High resolution project photos.
- No per-photo captions on gallery pages (text is separate).
- Use descriptive alt text + SEO filenames (service + location + detail).
- Hero often uses outdoor/living space photos.

## Other Elements
- Acuity Scheduling iframe (keep embed as-is initially).
- Carousel for testimonials.
- "Next / Previous" navigation between gallery pages.
- Mobile responsive, clean on small screens.

Replicate with Tailwind: Use lots of py-12, px-4/8, max-w-6xl mx-auto, font-semibold for headings, tracking-wide if uppercase feel needed. White bg, minimal borders.
